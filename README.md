# AI for Non-Traditional Industries

Exploring how AI can serve industries that have historically been left out of the tech conversation, not to replace human skill, but to extend it.

## POC 1 · AI + Indian Artisanship

**"Where AI Meets the Artisan's Hand"**

An interactive explainer covering six Indian craft traditions and how AI can help artisans without replacing what makes their work human.

Crafts covered:
- 🎨 Block Printing
- 🏺 Pottery & Ceramics
- 🧵 Textile Weaving
- 🪵 Woodwork & Carving
- 👗 Fashion & Craft Design
- 💙 Blue Pottery

Each card explores the problem artisans face, how AI could help, and what must always stay human.

Inspired by a visit to [IICD Jaipur](https://iicd.ac.in), where local artisans teach, learn, and keep India's craft traditions alive.

### Open it

**No install required** — just open [`index.html`](index.html) in any browser.

For developers, the React component source is in [`ai-crafts.jsx`](ai-crafts.jsx).

### Guiding principles

| Principle | Idea |
|---|---|
| AI as tool, not replacement | The artisan decides. AI assists. |
| Preserve the imperfection | Handmade means unique. That's the point. |
| Bridge, don't bypass | Connect artisans to markets without cutting them out. |
| Archive living knowledge | Traditions shouldn't die with their masters. |
